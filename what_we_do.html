Our Services

Event Management and Conference Logistics

In 2006, Symposia introduced its event management services and has since gained considerable capacity and experience in coordinating events and handling all the related logistics.

  • We can deliver a full package of event management services for small or large events (up to 1000 participants).
  • The event management package includes: Simultaneous Interpretation & Translation; Secretarial services (registration, computers, photocopiers and printers).
  • Branding & Advertising (design, production & printing of promotional materials, photo and video coverage of the event, decoration).
  • Conference Logistics (transport, venue identification and negotiation, hotel accommodation, ushers), Entertainment (cultural evenings, cocktails, dinners, concerts, post event tours) and overall Event Coordination.
Our Services

Simultaneous Interpretation and Translation

  • Symposia Consult offers high quality Simultaneous Interpretation and Translation services as well as rental of Simultaneous Interpretation Systems (SIS).
  • The conference interpreters and translators in our network are some of the most experienced in the regions we work in, guaranteeing a highly professional experience for all our clients.
  • All our Interpreters Booths and Simultaneous Interpretation Systems comply with ISO 2603 for Interpretation Systems.
  • We have the capacity to service conferences and meetings of up to 2000 participants and 7 languages.
